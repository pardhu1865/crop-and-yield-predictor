About the project

In today's agricultural landscape, the coexistence of traditional farming practices and advanced technology presents a critical challenge. Farmers, deeply rooted in tradition, often lack access to data-driven insights for crop selection and yield prediction, leading to suboptimal agricultural outcomes. Bridging this gap is essential. Therefore, the problem statement is to develop an accessible, scalable, and user-friendly agricultural decision support system that leverages data science and machine learning. This system should seamlessly integrate weather data, soil information, and crop yield statistics to provide farmers with actionable recommendations and yield predictions, empowering them to make informed decisions and maximize their harvests.
